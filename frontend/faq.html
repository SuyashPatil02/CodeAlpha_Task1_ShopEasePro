Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I place an order?

Browse the shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need an account to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) and online card payments. The online payment in this demo is simulated and never charges a real card.

How much is shipping?

Shipping is a flat $5, and completely free for orders over $50.

Can I track my orders?

Yes. Log in and visit "My Orders" to see the status of every order — pending, processing, shipped or delivered.

What is a wishlist?

The wishlist lets you save products to view or buy later. Just tap the ♥ icon on any product.

How do I return an item?

Contact our support team within 14 days of delivery and we'll guide you through the return process.
