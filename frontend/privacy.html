Privacy Policy

This page explains how ShopEase Pro handles your information in this demo application.

Information We Collect

When you create an account we store your name, email and an encrypted (hashed) password. When you place an order we store your shipping address and order details.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the store — authenticating your account, processing orders and showing your order history. We never sell your data.

Data Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text. Access to your account is protected with secure JSON Web Tokens (JWT).

Cookies & Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to keep your cart, wishlist and session token. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

Your Rights

You can update your profile at any time from your dashboard, and request account deletion by contacting support.
